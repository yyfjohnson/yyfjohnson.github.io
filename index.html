INTRODUCE

VAMK is located on the Palosaari university campus within a walking distance from Vaasa city centre. Technobothnia Education and Research Centre, Tritonia Academic Library, Design Centre Muova, and Alere, the learning centre of social services and health care are all located on Palosaari. The University of Vaasa and Novia University of Applied Sciences can also be found on the same campus.
